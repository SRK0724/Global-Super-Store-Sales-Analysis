# Global Super Store Sales Analysis: Driving Retail Success with Data

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes the **Global SuperStore** dataset to uncover actionable insights in sales performance, profit distribution, and customer behavior. Using Excel, an interactive dashboard was developed to help retail businesses make data-driven decisions.

## Business Problem

Retail organizations often face challenges in identifying top-performing markets, customer segments, and product categories. The main goal of this project is to provide clarity on sales trends, profitability, and seasonal fluctuations to guide strategic business decisions.

## Methodology

1. **Data Cleaning & Preparation:** Removed inconsistencies, handled missing values, and structured the dataset for analysis.
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Investigated sales, profit, and customer trends across markets, regions, segments, and categories.
3. **Dashboard Development:** Created pivot tables, charts, KPI cards, and interactive slicers in Excel to visualize insights effectively.
4. **Insight Generation:** Interpreted patterns to derive actionable business recommendations.

## Skills Used

* Microsoft Excel: Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers
* Data Cleaning and Preparation
* KPI Analysis and Dashboard Design
* Sales & Profit Trend Analysis
* Business Insights & Recommendations

## Results & Business Recommendation

* **Total Sales:** 12.6 Million, **Total Profit:** 1.5 Million
* **High-Performing Market:** APAC
* **Most Profitable Segment:** Consumer
